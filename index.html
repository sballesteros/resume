About me

I was trained as an engineer and agronomist but faced the delusion of so called agricultural development. I escaped and sought refuge in an ivory tower working in the theoretical world of statistics, mathematical modeling, epidemiology and theoretical ecology & evolution. There, I saw some infrastructural flaws and founded a company to change the way science is being published and used. Along the way, I became a versatile technologist with a solid experience in web development and turning ideas into products.
